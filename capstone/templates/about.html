About Me

Hi, I’m Jamis. I’m passionate about software development, with a particular love for creating computer games that entertain, inspire, and bring joy to others.

My journey into development has been shaped by curiosity, a self-taught mindset, and an unrelenting attention to detail. While life has thrown challenges my way, including a pivotal moment with Cancer, it also set me on the path to pursuing my dream of building games and software that make a difference.

I’ve always been fascinated by the power of technology—whether it’s AI, cutting-edge trends, or immersive gaming experiences—and I strive to stay at the forefront of the field. I’m currently focused on building games of small to medium scope, honing my skills, and collaborating with a talented animator friend to bring our dream game to life.

Ultimately, my goal is to create something that not only reflects my passion but also resonates with people around the world. I believe in the ability of games to tell stories, spark creativity, and connect communities—and I’m excited to contribute to that legacy.
